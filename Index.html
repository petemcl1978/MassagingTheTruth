About

Massaging the Truth is the education arm of Chris O'Neil Massage and Wellness Coaching. In over 12 years of practice I have come across so many challenges, concerns, and questions regarding massage therapy, body awareness, and self care. MTT is a searchable library of body awareness knowledge, self care techniques, and interesting trivia to empower you to have more control and understanding of how your body works, communicates, and what to do about it.
